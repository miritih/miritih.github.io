About Member Homes

Who we are:

We provide affordable, sustainable and quality housing and property development.

  • Vision
  • Mission
  • Values

To enable our members to acquire, make developments and generate wealth in preferred property investments everywhere in the country and beyond.

To accord members quality, affordable and sustainable property investments by presenting to them the best combination of land, location, finances and skills.

WHAT we do

Member Homes LLP is a scheme by Kenyan co-operators and other investment groups to assist ourselves in accessing property and other land investment opportunities across the country and beyond.

Our sole purpose is to bring to one market place all the land projects regardless of who or where they are, so that people can choose depending on their preference.

We are going further and partnering with other professional to add value is these investments by providing quicker transaction processing, home construction and mortgage loans for the members at very low rates.

We are making sure that each member of a Sacco (Chama, Church group etc.) or individual can access property at the place of their choice and hence has a realistic opportunity of developing it into a home or other investment activity.

HOW we do it

Saccos and other group investment companies are bring their projects together so that members can choose from the location, type and price that are favourable to them.

Member Homes LLP then processes that transaction to ensure everyone gets what they want.
